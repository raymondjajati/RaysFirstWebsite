Top 5 Rappers of All Time

1. Eminem

from Detroit, Michigan








Eminem is my favorite rapper because all his lyrics mean something. Whether it is about his life at home, or his rapping life most of his lyrics are based off true thingsthat happened to him. His real name is Marshall Bruce Mathers. He is also known as "The Slim Shady." His best songs are Till' I Collapse, lose Yourself and Mockingbird.

2. Drake

from Toronto, Canada








Drake has many songs that I enjoy very much. Drake is a rapper, singer, songwriter, record producer, and actor. His real name is Aubrey Drake Graham. His best songs are The Motto, Childs Play and Still here.

3. Kanye West

from Chicago, Illinois








Kanye Omari West is an American rapper, songwriter, record producer, fashion designer, and entrepreneur. His best songs are Blood on the Leaves, Forever and Heartless.

4. Tupac

from New York City








Tupac Shakur was born June 16, 1971. He was was an American rapper, record producer, actor, activist and poet. Tupac and B.I.G. were best friends until the competition in the rapping world got too competitive. His best songs are Only God Can Judge Me, I get Around and So Many Tears.

5. The Notorious B.I.G. (Biggie Smalls)

from Brooklyn, NY








The Notorious B.I.G. was an American rapper. He is consistently ranked as one of the greatest and most influential rappers of all time. His real name was Christopher George Latore Wallace. Biggie was killed by an unknown assailant in a drive-by shooting in Los Angeles. His double-disc album Life After Death, released 16 days later, rose to No. 1 on the U.S. album charts and was certified Diamond in 2000 by the Recording Industry Association of America, one of the few hip hop albums to receive this certification. His best songs are Suicidal Thoughts, Hyptonize and Get Money.
